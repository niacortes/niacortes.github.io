CEMEX Deutschland Site

CEMEX had just launched a new international portal, and they needed help to standardize the sites from individual countries. As a team, we followed an established structure and design guidelines and developed a set of templates that every country could match easily.
For CEMEX Germany, we were able to update the site following the new visual style and content format, as well as accommodate certain features to their particular business model.

Goal

To reorganize CEMEX Germany online information and align it to the new pattern.

My Role

UI Designer & Information Architect
